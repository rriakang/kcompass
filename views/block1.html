How did you make?

A prediction model was constructed by collecting and analyzing various data from Seoul National University Hospital,
Yongin Psychiatric Hospital, and Dongguk University Hospital.
This prediction model is used to predict a patient's future medical condition, diagnosis and treatment direction based on hospital patient data.
The constructed model was adapted by considering the characteristics and data characteristics of each hospital for efficient and accurate prediction.
Through this, it is expected to support better decision-making and patient care in the medical field.
